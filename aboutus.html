History of SERBERTO'S

SERBERTO’s started as a group project in partial fulfillment in the subject of entrepreneurship in Senior High School. The founder thought of the idea of conducting a business related to his interest which is ice cream. The SERBERTO’S business name was made on 10th of May 2023 and is named after the founder Robert Kenneth Valenzuela. Together with his groupmates worked together to develop the business. As a result, the idea had its business plan and after a few months, the project continued with the new set of members in college in the process of developing a website for the subject of Introduction to computing. After 4 years, SERBERTO’S then became an existing business and started its operation in its first branch in Makati City. The business continuously grows and develops as the time passes by and continues to learn and create products that give satisfaction to its customers.

The makers of SERBERTO’S believed that ice cream makes everything better. We envision our ice cream business as more than just a place to purchase and consume frozen desserts. We aim to create a warm and welcoming environment where people can gather, connect, and enjoy the simple pleasure of indulging in a sweet treat. By fostering an inclusive atmosphere, we hope to create a sense of community and provide a refuge from the stresses of everyday life.
